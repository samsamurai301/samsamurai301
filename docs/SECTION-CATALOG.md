# Shiftframe section and template catalog

## Signature art-direction sections

### Graphic Hero

The primary campaign opener. Choose Poster, Split, Centered, or Editorial Columns. Use an image, mobile image, Shopify-hosted video, or one of six native SVG motifs. Add edition index, eyebrow, rich heading, body copy, two actions, scroll prompt, metrics, rotating badge, and positioned product pins.

**Best for:** launches, seasonal edits, new brand introductions, collaborations, hero products.

### Brand Canvas

A graphic identity field with Diagonal, Centered, or Split composition; scalable native motif; and movable notes styled as card, tape, or outline.

**Best for:** campaign identity, brand principles, ingredient notes, studio messages, press snippets, event information.

### Product Atlas

An art-directed collection section with multiple asymmetric grid rhythms, up to twelve products, quick add, collection destination, and Poster, Catalog, Split Label, or Minimal cards.

**Best for:** current edit, best sellers, curated category, campaign assortment.

### Drop Room

A launch room with product or current-template product, image or motif, ticker, status, edition and quantity metadata, countdown/expired state, product form, facts, and optional email signup.

**Best for:** limited drops, preorder windows, collaborations, launch-day landing, event registration tied to a product.

### Story Chapters

Interactive chapter navigation pairs each story block with an image or native motif, caption, heading, body, statistic, and link. The visual state changes as visitors select or reach chapters.

**Best for:** origin, founder, process, ingredients, materials, technology, impact, care, or use cases.

### Bundle Studio

A native set builder. Product blocks can be preselected and include role, note, and motif. Selected available variants are totaled and added as separate cart lines in one request. Summary uses an independent color scheme.

**Best for:** routines, gift sets, complete looks, systems, sample combinations, “build the kit” merchandising.

### Specimen Grid

Mosaic, regular grid, or rail composition using Feature, Metric, Statement, and Image cards. Every card can use its own color scheme.

**Best for:** benefits, tests, commitments, ingredients, materials, service promises, principles, diagrams, and evidence.

### Image Reveal

Accessible before/after slider with real images or native motif fallbacks, labels, configurable starting position, frame color, and desktop height.

**Best for:** source-to-result, prototype-to-final, packaging reduction, process improvement, application, restoration, or visible performance.

### Proof Wall

Wall, Grid, or horizontal Rail with Quote, Metric, Press, and Badge blocks. Supports ratings, logos, attribution, source detail, and color-role variation.

**Best for:** verified reviews, named press, documented metrics, standards, guarantees, certifications, and partnerships.

## Core merchandising sections

### Featured Collection

Conventional collection grid with configurable count, columns, mobile rail, quick add, and “view all.” Use when a quieter rhythm is preferable to Product Atlas.

### Featured Product

Compact product presentation for any landing page. Includes app blocks, vendor, title, price, rating, variants, quantity, buy buttons, description, text, sharing, gift recipient fields, and Custom Liquid.

### Collection List

Merchant-selected collection cards with configurable desktop columns.

### Shoppable Lookbook

Large editorial image with positioned product hotspots.

### Product Comparison

Accessible product comparison table for up to four products, using text and semantic yes/no cells.

### Product Recommendations and Complementary Products

Native Shopify merchandising data rendered with Shiftframe product cards.

## Content and trust sections

- **Editorial Hero** — image/video hero for quieter layouts.
- **Rich Text** — block-based eyebrow, heading, copy, actions, app block, alignment, and width.
- **Image with Text** — two-column story or service panel.
- **Multicolumn** — image/icon columns for services, steps, values, or features.
- **FAQ** — accessible disclosure rows with optional first row open.
- **Featured Blog** — selected blog and configurable post count/excerpts.
- **Video** — Shopify-hosted or external video with cover image.
- **Logo List** — customer, retailer, press, partner, or certification logos.
- **Trust Strip** — concise icon-led service or policy statements.
- **Marquee** — repeated verbal brand code with speed and hover-pause controls.
- **Newsletter** — native customer signup with promise and disclaimer.
- **Contact Form** — native contact form with optional page content.
- **Custom Liquid** — controlled extension point for merchant/developer markup.

## Global systems

### Announcement Bar

Multiple messages, links, and color scheme in the header group.

### Header

Frame or centered layout; sticky option; menu; fallback/native brand mark; logo; promotional menu blocks; mobile navigation; search; account; cart; country; and language controls.

### Footer System

Manifesto or structured closing panel; mark/motif; newsletter; social; Follow on Shop; menu, text, and app blocks; policies; payment icons; and localization.

### Cart Drawer

Indexed title, native mark, motif-led empty state, line-item updates/removal, discounts, free-shipping progress, subtotal, and checkout actions.

### Search Dialog and Predictive Search

Full-screen indexed search composition with native motif, result status, products, queries, collections, pages, and articles.

## Main template sections

### Product Information

Gallery arrangements, sticky information, breadcrumbs, index, product badges, vendor, title, text, price, rating, SKU, inventory, variant picker, quantity, buy buttons, gift recipients, feature list, icon callouts, description, accordions, size/specification content, pickup, share, app blocks, and Custom Liquid.

### Collection Archive

Editorial Split, Poster Overlap, or Compact Index header; image/motif fallback; archive labels; native filtering/sorting; pagination; 2–5 columns; Regular, Modular, or Poster Wall grid; and four card languages.

### Search Archive

Graphic search opener, suggested routes, product/article/page results, filters, sorting, pagination, no-result guidance, editorial cards, and multiple grid/card treatments.

### Cart Archive

Motif-led indexed header, line-item rail, configurable summary color role, discounts, free-shipping progress, note, terms, accelerated checkout, and original empty state.

### Blog, Article, Page, Contact, Collections Index, 404, and Password

Complete core Shopify content routes with the global Shiftframe visual system.
